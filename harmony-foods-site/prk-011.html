PRK-011 — Delayed Response Syndrome

Classification: Internal / Behavioral Anomaly
Status: Escalated

Summary

Multiple subjects previously exposed to PRK compounds displayed delayed neurological response patterns following periods of non-exposure. Symptoms manifested without recent contact.

Observed Effects

  • Time-lagged motor impairment
  • Auditory processing delay
  • Involuntary micro-movements during rest states
  • Reported sensation of “anticipation without stimulus”

Notes

Initial assumption of withdrawal-related response was discarded after similar symptoms appeared in subjects with no reported craving or dependency markers.

Concern

This suggests the compound may establish latent interaction pathways independent of continued exposure. Further deployment without long-cycle studies is not advised.
