Harmony Foods

An in-universe interactive brand experience inside the Little Pricks universe.

Overview

Harmony Foods is a fictional company that exists inside the Little Pricks universe. Rather than functioning as a traditional corporate website, the site was designed as an explorable, narrative-driven experience.

The goal was to encourage discovery while maintaining the appearance of a legitimate corporate presence.

World Context

On the surface, Harmony Foods presents itself as wholesome and trustworthy. Beneath that surface are internal systems that suggest a much darker structure.

Interaction as Storytelling

Interactivity is used as a narrative device. Visitors uncover information by exploring rather than being guided.

Internal Systems

The internal archive shifts the tone from friendly branding to a monitored, bureaucratic environment.

Expanded Brand Ecosystem

Research divisions and partner brands reinforce the sense that Harmony Foods is only one piece of a much larger system.

Why This Project Matters

This project demonstrates how web design can function as narrative world-building, blurring the line between branding, storytelling, and play.
