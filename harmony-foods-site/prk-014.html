Executive Summary

With commercial trigger package finalized (PRK-013), Harmony Foods is prepared to scale PRICKLES production and distribute across partner trade corridors. Demand is expected to accelerate as broadcast saturation increases. In simpler terms: we press play, the galaxies snack.

Manufacturing Plan

  • Expand additive production capacity (liquid + powder forms)

  • Standardize “spice blend” variations so every bag hits the same craving curve

Distribution Plan

  • Initial roll-out: major hubs + “friendly neighbor lanes” (incl. Galaxy 9 trade corridor)
  • Secondary roll-out: remote colonies via long-haul pallets and partner co-packers

Projected Outcomes
