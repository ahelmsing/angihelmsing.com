PRK-007 — Botanical Cross-Response

Classification: Restricted Research
Status: Escalated

Summary

PRICKLES specimen placed near an office fern for observation.

Results

  • Fern curled inward toward specimen container
  • Soil temperature increased by 4°F
  • Fern later found uprooted, adjacent to specimen

Notes

Plant disposed of “with honors.” Do not allow specimen to remain unsupervised with decorative flora.

Secondary Interaction: Compound interaction observed beyond human subjects. Escalated to Product Integrity.
