PRK-009 — Cross Brand Contamination Event

Classification: Internal / Product Integrity
Status: Closed (Reclassified)

Summary

During routine flavor prep for Pebble Pops fruit line, a micro vial of PRK compound concentrate was introduced into Batch FP-77 by accidental contact during transfer. Incident occurred at Line 3, Station B, during shift change.

Immediate Actions

  • Batch FP-77 was flagged for hold, then released after sensory panel reported no off-notes
  • Sanitation protocol re-run on Line 3
  • Vial inventory audited, count reconciled after 46 minutes
  • Employee coaching completed, no further escalation

Observed Market Effects

Within 72 hours of distribution, FP-77 sell-through exceeded seasonal projections. Retail partners reported repeated restock requests and unusually high single-flavor preference.

  • Velocity exceeded baseline by 4.1x in pilot region
  • Customer feedback indicated "strong preference" without clear flavor descriptors
  • Adjacent flavors showed measurable preference drop during the same period

Internal Notes

Product Integrity confirms the compound concentration in FP-77 was below lowest lab threshold for structured response testing. Despite this, market behavior suggests an interaction with existing flavor architecture.

Recommendation: Document as a variance event. Do not attribute market response to formulation variance in external communications.

Origin Classification

Compound input lot traced to Nova Harvest Group import channel. Source documentation indicates legal intergalactic trade compliance. Origin region listed as Corridor 9. Secondary identifier references Galaxy 9 distribution hub.
