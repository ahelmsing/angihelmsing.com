PRK-006 — The Standing Incident

Classification: Internal / Behavioral Anomaly
Status: Closed

Summary

Three employees reported inability to sit for extended periods following vapor exposure.

Behavioral Notes

  • Subjects stated “the floor feels dishonest”
  • One claimed chairs were “watching”
  • Standing productivity increased 19%, morale decreased 42%

Conclusion

Not a safety issue. Update breakroom furniture policy to stools only.

Pattern Alert: Motor response anomalies observed following repeat low-dose exposure cycles.
